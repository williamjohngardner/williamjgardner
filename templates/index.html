Subasmbly: Final project for The Iron Yard

Subasmbly is a bill of materials application meant for Makers, hobbyists, and small businesses who previously relied on Excel spreadsheets for bill of materials management.

A gap in the market exists between Excel and enterprise level DBA systems like SAP and Subasmbly aims to provide part and revision management, as well as customer and supplier management tools to those who could not previously afford them.

Bil.ly

Bil.ly is a url shortener, written as part of the curriculum at The Iron Yard. Much like popular service Bit.ly, users can make profiles, log in, shorten urls, and save them to their profile.

Bil.ly incorporates Hash IDs as a secure way to encrypt urls.
